RECOVERY CONCEPT SPACE

STATUS
V0.2 implements bounded candidate/prior writing, inspection helpers and verified
prior cleanup.  A full visible recovery workspace and promotion interface remain
development work.

PURPOSE
Opening the app finds the strongest complete local authored body and makes every recovery choice understandable before changing files.

LIVE RECOVERY BODY
- A replacement save preserves a complete prior canonical body beside the final
  path and verifies the new final body after replacement.
- Inspection helpers enumerate the selected final, protected priors and sibling
  candidates within explicit entry, body and byte limits; each inspected body
  receives a complete-body SHA-256 and canonical parse result.
- Cleanup retains the newest two verified canonical priors by default, removes
  only older verified canonical priors, and holds uncertain bodies and diagnostic
  candidates.
- Open and save failures leave the live Editor adoption point unchanged and
  report their reason.

VISIBLE RECOVERY TARGET
Portray inspected bodies with exact path, byte extent, hash, generation and
authored summary; allow read-only opening and explicit promotion through the
same verified replacement flow.  No such promotion interface is attributed to
v0.2 yet.

CODE ASSOCIATIONS
SUPPORT.txt is associated with current inspection, bounded parsing and verified
cleanup.  EDITOR.txt and APP.txt are associated with the later visible recovery
and explicit promotion work.
