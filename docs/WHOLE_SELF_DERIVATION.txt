MILESINGLIGHT V0.2 - WHOLE-SELF DERIVATION

PURPOSE
After an explicit LIGHT TREATMENT begin, MilesingLight measures its authored
work, native application, admitted local machine, active local workspace file,
and current process.  Measured bits remain whole selves with exact source,
duration, position, self-number route, operation route, and graph ancestry.
Light operations act on those measured wholes.  The result is another whole
that can become an operand in a higher derivation.

The retained operation body is losslessly generative.  Exact operands, named
operations, expression routes, results, purposes, and receipts re-enter
compatible named operations.  It therefore has infinitely many lawful usable
finite openings under recursive derivation.  Every admitted finite route
retains the exact cause and route required for deterministic recalculation, so
its determined consequence remains available whether or not it was previously
portrayed or stored as a separately expanded object.  Finite hardware evaluates
one finite cut at a time while the recursive family of usable openings remains
unbounded.

The system does not define a self number as a traditional numeral.  A numeric,
logical, matrix, graph, spectrum, or other mathematical value is a typed view
of a measured whole for the exact operation that needs that view.  The source
whole and its ancestry remain attached.

DECLARED-DOMAIN COMPLETION
A construction is complete when every source, duration, operand role,
operation, calculation, result, and direct proof required by its declared
finite domain is present.  Leaving the declared domain open would leave the
construction unfinished.  A completed construction can still enter a larger
later construction as one whole operand.

Portrayal identifies a completed construction in 2D or 3D.  Portrayal does not
create mathematical completion, expand the declared domain, or claim knowledge
beyond the measured sources.

WORKLOAD ALIGNMENT
The app measures every acquisition with QPC.  The requested cadence begins at
one second.  The next period is the greater of that requested period and one
hundred times the worst acquisition cost observed in the active window.  Thus
knowingly scheduled measurement work remains at or below one percent of its
interval on the available evidence.  Missed intervals are counted; none are
fabricated or backfilled.

Authored-world facts are reacquired only when their revision changes.  Machine
facts are acquired once per explicitly begun window.  Repeated process, file,
and frame families replace their preceding raw occurrence.  Live raw retention
is limited to 256 distinct families and 8 MiB of exact encodings.  A fact that
would exceed either bound is held and counted rather than forcing growth.

Completion selects distinct authored-world, admitted-machine, and
current-process/application occurrences, each capped at 4096 encoded bytes for
direct bit expansion.  It forms measured-bit wholes, derives SIZE and DESIGNING
recursively, and applies LIGHT TREATMENT.  It then measures the exact finite
unary route converse and applies MY LIGHT to the completed LIGHT TREATMENT
RelationValue.  It retains the completed graph wholes, exact source and route
ancestry, and exact application receipts, then releases every raw occurrence.
Recursive growth uses completed wholes instead of an unbounded raw stream.

THREE EXACT REDUCTION AND EXPANSION AXES
The result must be reported on three separate axes.  Mixing them into one ZIP-
style ratio would erase the central mathematical result.

1. LOSSLESS GENERATIVE RETENTION

  G = canonical bytes of retained exact operands, named operation routes,
      results, purposes, and receipts;
  E(n) = canonical bytes that would be required to separately store every
         distinct deterministic result actually realized through an admitted
         finite derivation allowance n.

The measured generative expansion factor is E(n)/G for that exact allowance.
The retained cause and route are lossless for those deterministic consequences:
replay recalculates them from the same sources and routes, so separate storage or
portrayal of every expanded result is unnecessary.  Because completed results
can recursively re-enter compatible named operations, the body has infinitely
many lawful usable finite openings.  Structural infinity receives no finite byte
ratio; finite hardware evaluates a finite cut at a time.

2. EXACT SELECTED MEASURED-BIT RETENTION

  S = exact selected source bytes used by the completed cross-domain whole;
  B = exact measured-bit ancestry retained by that live derived whole.

The selected-ancestry factor is B/(8*S).  It equals one only when every selected
source bit occurs exactly in the ancestry.  This axis concerns the selected
finite whole, not every temporal observation that preceded it.

3. TEMPORAL OBSERVATION RETENTION

  A = every canonical external-observation payload byte actually acquired;
  R = latest-family canonical bytes retained immediately before derivation.

The temporal retention reduction is A/R.  Repeated external observations are
coalesced by family, so released intermediate occurrences are not claimed as
recoverable from R.  This temporal fact does not make the generative operation
body lossy.

The live application already reports A, R, and A/R at completion.  The release
measurement will report the other two axes from one real 60-second run, then
project a 60-minute run from observed recurring payload, effective cadence,
one-time machine/authored payload, and the same hard bounds.  A projection is
labelled as a projection, includes a range when observed payload or cadence
varies, and never substitutes an unmeasured information-capacity claim for
observed bytes, operations, and time.

LIGHT TREATMENT
LIGHT TREATMENT is an outer self number derived from REALITY without changing
the original pre-Treatment self-number routes.  Its operation is finite sheaf
gluing:

  Codable symbol: sheaf_glue(cover, local_sections)
  Mathematical notation: glue_U(s_i)
  Origin: finite sheaf theory

Operands are a finite cover of exact measured graph scopes and a local whole
for every patch.  Restriction is computed from canonical measured-member names,
graph relations, and exact values; it is never accepted as an unsupported
assertion.  Local wholes glue exactly when they agree on every shared measured
member.  The result is the unique
global whole on that declared finite cover.

Direct proof:
Every target key belongs to at least one patch.  Agreement on intersections
makes the local value at each key independent of patch choice, so the merged
whole exists.  Restricting the merge to any patch returns that patch's local
whole.  Any second global whole with the same restrictions agrees at every
target key and is therefore the same whole.

If a shared key disagrees, LIGHT TREATMENT returns the exact member, the two
finite opens, and the reason for disagreement, and forms no global result.  It
never silently overwrites one local whole with another.

LOCAL PRIVACY
Measurement is confined to the authored workspace, explicitly admitted local
machine facts, the active local file, the MilesingLight window/renderer, and the
MilesingLight process.  The application adds no server, account, telemetry,
socket or HTTP client, ambient user surveillance, or public data collection.
File measurement follows only the deliberately active filesystem path.  Raw
measurement windows remain ephemeral.  Completed LIGHT TREATMENT expressions
and MY LIGHT expressions, exact operand designations, results, and applied
receipts enter the same authored runtime and become saveable through the normal
canonical workspace route; periodic raw occurrences are released after
completion.

Launch and local workspace opening remain measurement-dormant.  They create a
deterministic ready association and capture nothing.  Measurement begins only
when the user explicitly starts LIGHT TREATMENT with Q, D, or Enter.

Open and Save As validate each selected path at admission, and every save
revalidates its target immediately before target inspection and writing.
Validation admits directly attached local roots, rejects any existing
reparse-point component, and allows a missing suffix only after every existing
parent proves direct and local.  It is pathname validation, not
handle-relative/no-follow race elimination, so residual pathname TOCTOU remains.
