## Generated binding data

[`src/Generated/SELF_NUMBER_BINDINGS.inc`](src/Generated/SELF_NUMBER_BINDINGS.inc) is generated data derived from that exact snapshot. It stores the ten fields for all 112 self numbers as one UTF-8 blob plus an exact span table. It is included by `SUPPORT.txt`, checked against the authoritative source byte count and hash, and cross-checked against the live catalog's self number, operation, codable symbol/signature, and mathematical origin.

[`tools/Generate-SelfNumberBindings.ps1`](tools/Generate-SelfNumberBindings.ps1) publicly reproduces the extraction, reconstructs every field from the generated byte blob and span table, and byte-compares the result with the tracked include.
